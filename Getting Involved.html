Getting Involved

FITSEC is a competitive collegiate Women In Computing club created to provide the students of Florida Tech with a competitive team full of members willing to help push the technical bounds of every member to the next level. The first requirement for anyone to join FITSEC is to be a currently enrolled student at Florida Tech. Discord: Join us on discord! We put out details about meetings and upcoming competitions through discord. Enroll in Engage: Florida Tech uses campus labs as its student organization portal. Please join our engage portal so that Florida Tech can track all of the members.
